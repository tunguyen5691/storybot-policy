Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Story Teller ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Story Teller, we may collect the following information:

  • TikTok Account Data: Basic profile information (username, display name) required for authentication via TikTok OAuth
  • Content Data: Videos and metadata you choose to upload through our application
  • Usage Data: Logs of actions performed within the app for debugging and service improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and connect to your TikTok account
  • Upload and publish videos to TikTok on your behalf
  • Improve the performance and reliability of our service

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. TikTok API Data

Story Teller accesses your TikTok account through the official TikTok API. We request only the minimum permissions necessary to provide the service:

  • video.upload — to upload video files to your account
  • video.publish — to publish or schedule posts

We do not store your TikTok access tokens beyond the active session. You can revoke our app's access at any time through your TikTok account settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We do not permanently store your video content — files are processed in memory and transmitted directly to TikTok.

5. Data Retention

We retain minimal data necessary to operate the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will delete all stored information associated with your account within 30 days.

6. Third-Party Services

Story Teller integrates with TikTok's API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

Story Teller is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request data deletion, please contact us at: support@storyteller.app
